FAQ

Questions we get
asked a lot.

How long does it take to build my website?

+

Most websites are delivered within 2–3 weeks from the discovery call. More complex projects with full AI automation systems typically take 3–4 weeks. We'll give you a clear timeline before we start.

Do I need any technical knowledge?

+

None at all. We handle everything — design, development, and automation setup. Once it's live, you just open your email or spreadsheet and your leads are there. No tech skills required.

What happens after the website is live?

+

On the Growth and Scale plans, we monitor performance monthly and send you a report showing leads captured, conversion rates, and what we're improving. On Starter, we offer ongoing support at an hourly rate.

Can you work with my existing website?

+

Yes. We can add AI automation and lead capture systems to your existing site without rebuilding it from scratch. Book a free audit and we'll assess what's possible.

Is there a contract or lock-in period?

+

No lock-in contracts. The one-time fee covers the build, and monthly plans can be cancelled anytime with 30 days notice. We keep clients because of results, not contracts.

What industries do you work with?

+

We specialise in local service businesses — gyms, clinics, barbershops, real estate agencies, trades, cafes, and more. If you rely on local customers, we can help.
